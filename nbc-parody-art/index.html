Overview

Saturday Night Live and Late Night with Seth Meyers often feature sketch comedy that parodies pop culture. Our design team makes parody movie posters, album art, and everything in between as a fun way to complement high-performing clips. For SNL, we have 1-4 hours to make each piece. These are some that I made.
