--- layout: portfolio title: A Little Late with Lilly Singh ---

Overview

I led digital design for the second season of A Little Late. Season 2 is more true to Lilly Singh's personality, so we refreshed digital and social assets to feel more vibrant, while still conveying Lilly as a disruptor of late-night television. Some of these graphics were made by other designers under my supervision.

Tools Used

Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects
